🌱 Plant Care Guide 🌱

Your green friends deserve the best care! 💚

🌺 Tropical & Foliage Plants

Pink Princess Philodendron

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light. Avoid direct sun.
  • 💧Water: Let top inch of soil dry before watering.
  • 💨Humidity: Prefers moderate to high humidity.

Calathea (roseopicta)

  • ☀️Light: Low to medium indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil consistently moist. Use distilled water.
  • 💨Humidity: High humidity is essential.

Rattlesnake Plant

  • ☀️Light: Low to medium indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil consistently moist.
  • 💨Humidity: High humidity is essential.

Croton

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil moist but not soggy.
  • 💨Humidity: High humidity preferred.

White Knight Philodendron

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Let top inch of soil dry before watering.
  • 💨Humidity: Moderate to high humidity.

Snake Plant

  • ☀️Light: Low to bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry completely between watering.
  • 🌱Care: Very tolerant. Avoid overwatering.

Monstera Deliciosa

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Support: Stake or plank for support.

Polka Dot Plants

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil lightly moist.
  • ✂️Care: Pinch back regularly to prevent legginess.

Tradescantia (Rainbow variety)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil slightly moist.
  • ✂️Care: Pinch tips to maintain fullness.

Bromeliad

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep water in central 'cup'.
  • 🌱Care: Flush cup weekly to prevent buildup.

Orchid (Phalaenopsis)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water weekly in bark mix.
  • 🌱Care: Avoid wetting crown.

🌵 Succulents & Cacti

Zebra Haworthia & Haworthia Cooperi

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct light.
  • 💧Water: Allow soil to dry completely.
  • 🌱Soil: Use a gritty, well-draining soil mix.

Cactus & Echeveria

  • ☀️Light: Full sun.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry out completely.
  • ❄️Placement: Avoid cold drafts.

Assorted Cactus

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct light.
  • 💧Water: Minimal water needed.
  • 🌱Care: Ensure excellent drainage.

Propeller Plant

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct sun.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry fully; avoid overwatering.
  • 💨Humidity: Prefers low humidity.

String of Pearls

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect to direct light.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry fully between waterings.
  • 🏺Potting: Use shallow pots with good drainage.

Aloe Vera

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct light.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry completely.
  • ❄️Placement: Avoid cold or drafty areas.

💚 Happy planting! Your plants will thank you with beautiful growth! 💚
