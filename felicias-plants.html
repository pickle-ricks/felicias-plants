🌱 Felicia's Plant Care Guide 🌱

🌺 Tropical & Foliage Plants

Pink Princess Philodendron

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect (more light maintains pink).
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1–2" dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate (50% ideal).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Give a moss pole; prune green shoots to preserve variegation.

Calathea roseopicta ‘Dottie’

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect; no direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep evenly moist, never soggy; small, regular drinks; half-strength foliage feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled/RO: strongly preferred.
  • 💦Humidity: High (60–70%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Self-watering/globe can help; warm, draft-free.

Calathea roseopicta ‘Medallion’

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Evenly moist; light, frequent waterings; half-strength monthly in season; Distilled/RO: strongly preferred.
  • 💦Humidity: High (60–70%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Globe/self-watering ok; avoid cold leaves.

Rattlesnake Plant (Calathea/Goeppertia lancifolia)

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep evenly moist; light, frequent waterings; half-strength monthly in season; Distilled/RO: strongly preferred.
  • 💦Humidity: High (60–70%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Globe/self-watering helpful; warm, no drafts.

Croton (Codiaeum variegatum)

  • ☀️Light: Bright light; a few hours direct sun keeps color.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep evenly moist; don’t let fully dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Higher is better (50–60%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Hates change/drafts—keep in one stable, bright spot.

White Knight Philodendron

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect; avoid strong direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1–2" dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate (50% ideal).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Provide a stake/moss pole; rotate for even variegation.

Monstera deliciosa

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect; tolerates some gentle morning sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 2–3" dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate (40–60%).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Loves a moss pole/trellis for bigger fenestrations.

Swiss Cheese Monstera (Monstera adansonii)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1–2" dry; balanced feed monthly in season; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate–high.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Will climb; attach to a pole for larger leaves.

Polka Dot Begonia (Begonia maculata)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect; avoid harsh sun on leaves.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1" just dries; begonia-safe fertilizer 1/2-strength monthly; Distilled/RO: helpful.
  • 💦Humidity: Higher is better (50–60%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Stake canes; prune for fullness; avoid wet leaves at night.

Polka Dot Plant – pink (Hypoestes)

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Evenly moist; light feed every 2–4 wks; Distilled: optional.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate–high.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Pinch; globe ok.

Polka Dot Plant – white (Hypoestes)

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Evenly moist; light feed every 2–4 wks; Distilled: optional.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate–high.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Pinch; globe ok.

Wandering Dude (Tradescantia zebrina)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect to gentle direct.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1" dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Average–moderate.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Pinch frequently for bushiness; easy to root cuttings.

Neoregelia Bromeliad

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect, some gentle sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep central cup filled with fresh water; keep soil just barely moist; very dilute fertilizer occasionally in cup; Distilled/RO: preferred to avoid mineral spotting.
  • 💦Humidity: High (60%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Flush cup monthly to prevent stagnation.

Moth Orchid (Phalaenopsis)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect (no hot sun).
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when bark is nearly dry/roots turn silvery (about weekly); 1/4-strength orchid fertilizer “weakly weekly”; Distilled/RO: fine if you fertilize (adds Ca/Mg).
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate (40–60%).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Soak-drain method; never leave water in crown.

Pink Moth Orchid (Phalaenopsis, pink cultivar)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when bark nearly dry; 1/4-strength orchid fertilizer “weakly weekly”; Distilled/RO: fine with fertilizer.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Soak-drain; avoid crown water.

Golden Pothos (Epipremnum aureum)

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect; tolerates low.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1–2" dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Average.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Trailing or climbing; extremely forgiving.

Silver Pothos (Scindapsus pictus)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect (keeps silvery variegation).
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1–2" dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Trailing/climbing; avoid strong midday sun.

Lucky Bamboo (Dracaena sanderiana)

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect; no direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: If in water: keep roots submerged, change water weekly, add a drop of hydroponic fertilizer monthly; Distilled/RO: preferred (fluoride/chlorine sensitive).
  • 💦Humidity: Average.
  • 🌱Additional Info: If potted, keep evenly moist, never soggy.

Bird of Paradise (Strelitzia)

  • ☀️Light: Very bright light; several hours of direct sun best.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1–2" dry; flush pot thoroughly; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Average–moderate.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Big pot, strong light = faster growth; natural leaf splits are normal.

Rubber Plant (Ficus elastica)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect; some gentle morning sun ok.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top half of pot is dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required (wipe leaves if hard-water spots).
  • 💦Humidity: Average.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Likes bright, stable spot; rotate for even growth.

Green Dragon Alocasia (A. baginda group)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep slightly moist; don’t let fully dry; half-strength monthly in warm months; Distilled/RO: helpful (can be mineral-sensitive).
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate–high (50–65%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Warmth and consistent moisture; dormant spells possible.

Piper crocatum

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect, no direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep evenly moist (never dry or soggy); light feed monthly; Distilled/RO: preferred.
  • 💦Humidity: High (70–90%).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Best in a closed terrarium/dome; warm and draft-free.

Silver Splash Hoya (Hoya, vining)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect to a few hours soft direct.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water thoroughly, then dry most of the way; bloom-boosting fertilizer lightly in season; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate (40–60%).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Blooms when root-bound and bright; don’t remove peduncles.

Spider Plant (Chlorophytum comosum)

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1–2" dry; balanced feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled/RO: helpful (fluoride sensitive).
  • 💦Humidity: Average–moderate.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Sends runners (“spiderettes”); self-watering ok if airy mix.

Variegated Creeping Fig (Ficus pumila ‘Variegata’)

  • ☀️Light: Bright–medium indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep evenly moist; light feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Higher is better (50%+).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Clinging vine—can attach to surfaces; prune to contain.

Watermelon Peperomia (Peperomia argyreia)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water when top 1" dry; light feed monthly in season; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate (50%+ ideal).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Thick leaves store water—avoid overwatering; no globe.

🌵 Succulents & Cacti

Echeveria elegans

  • ☀️Light: Full sun/very bright light.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Soak then bone-dry; cactus feed 1/4-strength monthly growing season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Rosette must stay dry; no globe.

Ghost Echeveria (Echeveria ‘Lilacina’/similar)

  • ☀️Light: Full sun/very bright.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Dry fully between deep waterings; weak cactus feed monthly in season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Great drainage; no globe.

Baby Toes Cactus (Fenestraria)

  • ☀️Light: Very bright light; a few hours direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Thorough soak, then dry fully; water sparingly in winter; cactus feed very weak monthly in growing season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Deep pot with gritty mix; avoid globe.

Bunny Ear Cactus (Opuntia microdasys)

  • ☀️Light: Full sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Soak then dry completely; very little in winter; cactus feed 1/4-strength monthly warm season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Don’t touch—tiny glochids; no globe.

Gymnocalycium Cactus

  • ☀️Light: Very bright light; some direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Deep soak then fully dry; minimal in winter; weak cactus feed monthly warm season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Likes a cool, dry winter rest.

Ladyfinger Cactus (Mammillaria elongata)

  • ☀️Light: Full sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Soak-dry cycle; very sparse in winter; weak cactus feed in season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Compact, offsets readily; no globe.

Spiny Pincushion Cactus (Mammillaria sp.)

  • ☀️Light: Full sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Soak-dry; very light in winter; weak cactus feed in season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Excellent drainage; no globe.

String of Pearls (Curio/Senecio rowleyanus)

  • ☀️Light: Very bright light; a few hours direct sun indoors.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Water thoroughly, then dry fully; sparse in winter; succulent feed 1/4-strength monthly warm months; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Use shallow, free-draining pot; avoid globe/self-watering.

String of Turtles (Peperomia prostrata)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect (no hot sun).
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Keep lightly moist—never sopping; let top 1/2" dry; very light feed monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not required.
  • 💦Humidity: Moderate (50%+ ideal).
  • 🌱Additional Info: Fine roots—use airy mix; avoid globe.

Aloe vera

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct sun 4–6h (or very bright indirect).
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Let soil dry completely; deep water every 2–4 wks; cactus feed 1/4-strength monthly spring–summer; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Very fast-draining gritty mix; no globes/self-watering.

Panda Plant (Kalanchoe tomentosa)

  • ☀️Light: Bright light, some direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Dry fully; sparse water; weak cactus feed monthly warm season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Hairy leaves—avoid misting; no globe.

Paddle Plant (Kalanchoe thyrsiflora/luciae)

  • ☀️Light: Full sun/very bright light (color improves with sun).
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Dry fully between waterings; cactus feed 1/4-strength monthly warm season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Gritty mix; no globe.

Coral Cactus / “Mermaid Tail” (Euphorbia lactea cristata, grafted)

  • ☀️Light: Bright light with some direct sun.
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Allow full dry between waters; very light cactus feed monthly warm season; Distilled: not needed.
  • 💦Humidity: Low.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Avoid cold and overwatering; latex sap is toxic; no misting/globe.

🐠 Aquatic & Fish Tank Plants

Fishtank Plants (pothos/lucky bamboo/tradescantia in aquarium)

  • 💡Light: Bright–medium indirect (leaves above water).
  • 💧Water & Fertilizer: Rinse roots occasionally; do not add houseplant fertilizer to tank—rely on fish waste or fish-safe nutrients; Distilled: not necessary if tank is stable.
  • 💦Humidity: Ambient.
  • 🌱Additional Info: Keep only roots submerged; leaves/stems above waterline.
