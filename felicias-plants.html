🌺 Tropical & Foliage Plants

Pink Princess Philodendron

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Let top inch of soil dry before watering.
  • 🌱Notes: Prefers moderate to high humidity. Avoid direct sunlight to preserve variegation.

Calathea (roseopicta)

  • ☀️Light: Low to medium indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil consistently moist, not soggy.
  • 💨Notes: High humidity is essential. Use distilled water to avoid leaf browning.

Rattlesnake Plant

  • ☀️Light: Low to medium indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil consistently moist.
  • 💨Notes: High humidity is essential.

Croton

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil moist but not soggy.
  • 💨Notes: High humidity preferred. Sensitive to drafts and low light.

White Knight Philodendron

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Let top inch of soil dry before watering.
  • 🌱Notes: Moderate to high humidity. Wipe leaves occasionally.

Snake Plant

  • ☀️Light: Low to bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry completely between watering.
  • 🌱Notes: Very tolerant. Avoid overwatering.

Monstera Deliciosa

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Likes moderate humidity. Stake or plank for support.

Polka Dot Plants

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil lightly moist.
  • ✂️Notes: High humidity preferred. Pinch back regularly to prevent legginess.

Tradescantia (Rainbow variety)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil slightly moist.
  • ✂️Notes: Medium humidity. Pinch tips to maintain fullness.

Bromeliad

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep water in central 'cup'.
  • 🌱Notes: Flush cup weekly to prevent buildup. Moderate to high humidity.

Orchid (Phalaenopsis)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water weekly in bark mix.
  • 🌱Notes: Avoid wetting crown. High humidity preferred.

Pothos

  • ☀️Light: Low to bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Very tolerant. Can grow in water or soil. Trim to maintain shape.

Bird of Paradise

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect to direct light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil moist but not soggy.
  • 🌱Notes: High humidity preferred. Wipe leaves regularly. Needs space to grow.

Rubber Plant

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Wipe leaves regularly. Moderate humidity. Can grow quite large.

Alocasia Green Dragon

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.
  • 💨Notes: High humidity essential. Sensitive to temperature changes.

Monstera Adansonii

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Provide climbing support. Moderate to high humidity.

Piper Crocatum (Glass Globe)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil moist but not soggy.
  • 💨Notes: High humidity required. Beautiful silver markings on leaves.

Dracaena Fragrans (Jessina)

  • ☀️Light: Low to medium indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Very tolerant. Use distilled water to prevent leaf browning.

Scindapsus Silver Exotica (Silver Pothos)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Moderate humidity. Beautiful silver variegation on leaves.

Dischidia Ovata (Hanging)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when soil is almost dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Epiphytic plant. High humidity preferred. Good for hanging baskets.

Red Vein Maranta (Prayer Plant)

  • ☀️Light: Low to medium indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Keep soil consistently moist.
  • 💨Notes: High humidity essential. Leaves fold at night like praying hands.

Spider Plant Vittatum

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Very easy to care for. Produces plantlets for propagation.

Peperomia Rotundifolia (Trailing Jade)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Moderate humidity. Great for hanging baskets or trailing displays.

Peperomia Argyreia (Watermelon Plant)

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect light.
  • 💧Water: Water when top inch of soil is dry.
  • 🌱Notes: Moderate humidity. Distinctive watermelon-striped leaves.

🌵 Succulents & Cacti

Zebra Haworthia & Haworthia Cooperi

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct light.
  • 💧Water: Allow soil to dry completely between waterings.
  • 🌱Notes: Low humidity. Use a gritty, well-draining soil mix.

Cactus & Echeveria

  • ☀️Light: Full sun.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry out completely before watering.
  • ❄️Notes: Very low humidity. Avoid cold drafts.

Assorted Cactus

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct light.
  • 💧Water: Minimal water.
  • 🌱Notes: Ensure excellent drainage. Rotate to promote even growth.

Propeller Plant

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct sun.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry fully.
  • 💨Notes: Low humidity. Avoid misting or overwatering.

String of Pearls

  • ☀️Light: Bright indirect to direct light.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry fully between waterings.
  • 🏺Notes: Low humidity. Use shallow pots with drainage.

Aloe Vera

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct light.
  • 💧Water: Let soil dry completely before watering.
  • ❄️Notes: Low humidity. Avoid cold or drafty areas.

Kalanchoe Tomentosa (Panda Plant)

  • ☀️Light: Bright direct light.
  • 💧Water: Water deeply but infrequently.
  • 🐾Notes: Fuzzy, velvety leaves. Low humidity. Very drought tolerant.

🐠 Aquatic & Fish Tank Plants

Pothos (Aquatic)

  • 💡Light: Aquarium lighting or bright indirect light.
  • 🐠Water: Roots submerged in fish tank water.
  • 🌱Notes: Helps filter tank water. Keep leaves above waterline.

Bamboo (Aquatic)

  • 💡Light: Low to medium aquarium lighting.
  • 🐠Water: Grows well in fish tank water.
  • 🌱Notes: Actually Dracaena sanderiana. Excellent for aquariums.

Tradescantia (Aquatic)

  • 💡Light: Bright aquarium lighting.
  • 🐠Water: Roots in water, leaves above surface.
  • ✂️Notes: Fast growing. Trim regularly to maintain shape.
